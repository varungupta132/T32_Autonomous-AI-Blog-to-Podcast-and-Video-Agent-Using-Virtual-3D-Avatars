## 💡 Pro Tips

### Best Results Ke Liye:

#### Podcast Generation:
- Clear, well-formatted text use karo
- Speaker labels add karo (Host:, Guest:)
- Paragraphs reasonable length mein rakho
- Hinglish naturally use karo

#### Animated Player:
- Clear audio upload karo (MP3, WAV)
- File size < 50MB recommended
- Good audio quality = better experience

#### FAL Avatar Videos:
- Audio length: 30 seconds - 5 minutes ideal
- Clear speech best hai
- Avatar ko voice gender se match karo
- 2-3 minutes wait karo generation ke liye
